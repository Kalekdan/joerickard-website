Holiday Home Near the Beach in Brittany.

Welcome to the Website for our holiday home which is within walking/cycling distance of the beaches of southern Brittany.

The house is situated close to the fishing port and historic town of Concarneau and to the artists village of Pont Aven.

It is located in the residential hamlet of Lambell - midway between the town of Tregunc (1 mile away) and the beaches of southern Brittany (1 mile away).

Tregunc has a population of about 6000 people and has a wide range of shops, supermarkets (some open on Sundays) and sports facilities. There is a weekly market in the town centre.

A local bar/restauraunt and small shop are a short stroll from the house.

The house can accomodate up to 8 people and is set in a large garden overlooking fields to the south.
