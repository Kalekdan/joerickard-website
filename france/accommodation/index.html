The house can accommodate up to 8 people and is spread over 3 floors: The ground floor consists of a large garage and basement area. Here we keep various outdoor and indoor games (including a full size table tennis table), various adult and childrens bikes and, on a more practical level, there is a washing machine, sink and clothes line.

There is a door giving access to the Garden at the rear and a garge door to the front.

The first (entrance) floor is accessed up a flight of steps on the outside but there is an internal staircase from the basement as well.

On this floor is the hallway off which there is a large sitting/dining/kitchen. The dining table seats 8 and there are armchairs and a sofa bed (sleeps 2). On this floor there is also a double bedroom as well as the bathroom and separate wc.

The second floor is accessed up a flight of stairs where there are two further bedrooms. One has two single beds and the second has a pair of bunkbeds.
